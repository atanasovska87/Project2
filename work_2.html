Vintage Co

Vintage Co is a newly established company in the area of preserving the national vintage stuff. The team is constantly working on exploring new areas around the country.

The new campaign includes all millenials and has some pretty exciting stuff to offer. Read about the challenge we had below.


Challenge

Vintage Co came to us with the objective to create a powerful imagery for their new campaign. They knew exactly what they want, but had no idea how to accomplish that. The brand guidelines had to be kept, but new ideas were always accepted.

Insight

We knew they would want to keep the vintage feel throughtout the whole campaign. They are innovative and creative, yet conservative and authentic. Their target are millenials, free-spirited and with a mind-set that always thinks out of the box.

Idea

Localized had the idea to deliver 100 vintage cameras to a specific focus group and give them a week to take snaps. The idea was to take photos whenever and wherever they want, but in the duration of one week.

Result

Seems like everyone is creative if they are left with just a simple tool and their imagination. We received tons of wonderful photos we edited and about 30% of those were also used for Vintage Co's global campaign along with the ones taken by our team.
