The Coffee Shop

A truly great coffee shop has to feel special. Whether you want to enjoy a chat with a friend, meet with a co-worker or simply enjoy our relaxed atmosphere and friendly staff, we invite you to come and experience Coffee Co.

Coffee Co offers ambiance with no pretense, friendly baristas, delicious homemade food and of course, our legendary coffee. We believe in what a cup of coffee can do to enrich lives.


Challenge

What we had to do for the Coffee Company was to accelerate their brand presence. They already had an established brand identity, but wanted to reach a broader audience.

Insight

At Coffee Co, the food is as good as their coffee! The menu is loaded with crowd favorite classics, homemade soups, signature sandwiches and salads that make us more than Lancasters Best Coffee Experience.

Idea

The idea was to promote the food as well as the coffee. We establihed a sub-brand, from a compatible brand identity, to a full brand book about the sub brand. We had so much fun doing this.

Result

We were amazed by how much word-of-mouth played a role, once the brand was established. Once people tried the great food, it was hard not to pass the experience on to someone else. We had great results with 100% return on the investment we made.
